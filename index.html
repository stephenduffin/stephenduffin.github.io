Welcome!

This is my portfolio.

It is built from free HTML code on HTML5up, and has been adapted accordingly. It is a work in progress...

I have a passion for tech, mostly working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm teaching myself Python and SQL. I also have basic knowledge of Scrum, Agile, and Cloud Computing.

I am currently building a fantasy coffee shop website. This will feature images of disco glitter, white Persian cats, and faded movie stars.

The invisible coffee tastes fabulous - each cup accompanied by a free invisible macaron.

Payment can be made with either pink unicorns, purple frogs, or speckled blue hedgehogs.

All slices of the invisible, zero-calorie chocolate cake are free!
