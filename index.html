Welcome!

Here's my portfolio. As you can see, it's a work in progress...

I have a passion for tech, mostly working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm teaching myself back-end technologies including node.js and mongoDB. I also have basic knowledge of Scrum, Agile, and MS Azure.

My work includes sites made with Wix and Wordpress as listed below:

  • The Kitsch Coffee Lounge - A fictional coffee shop selling tempting treats. Take your pick of freshly-made cakes and macarons.
  • Brunswick Square Gardens - A photogallery showing images of the square in all seasons.
  • FractalForce - A photogallery showing a few of the stunning fractal images that can be made in fractal generators.
  • Fibonacci Numbers - An introduction to the fibonacci sequence and how it works.
  • A Wordpress blogsite linking to my published Flash Fiction.
